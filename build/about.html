Hi!

Glad you found my little corner of the internet! I am a developer who occasionally bakes bread, buns and other items in his spare time. This page exists because I often use elsewhere on the internet for recipes, and I felt there are no pages that work as I please

Most other sites are full of advertisements and life stories. They are also difficult to read, since you have to shift the focus between the ingredient list and the instructions while baking. In the end, they also do not make it easy for me to scale the recipes, if, for example, I have a few eggs in the fridge, or I want to bake three loaves of bread instead of two

What's different here?

This page consists of some of my own recipes, and many that are sourced from elsewhere on the internet. The difference is that I have remove all unnecessary text, made it possible to scale per ingredient, and added pre-scaled ingredients inside the text

The scaling works so that you can either scale the entire recipe, or scale based on individual ingredients. So if you want to make waffles but only have two eggs, then you can still use the same recipe, just scaled down to two eggs.

Each ingredient in the recipe text also has a checkbox, so you can too check the ingredients in the text as you use them. This way you will not miss any ingredients

If the recipe is taken from another place, I have tried to be good at linking back to the original recipe.
